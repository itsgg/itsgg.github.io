Discover tranquility in ten minutes.

<!--more-->

1. Find a quiet spot.
2. Set a timer for 10 minutes.
3. Sit comfortably, keeping your back straight.
4. Close your eyes.
5. Pay attention to your breathing.
6. If your thoughts drift, calmly return to focusing on your breath.
7. When the timer goes off, open your eyes slowly and look around.
